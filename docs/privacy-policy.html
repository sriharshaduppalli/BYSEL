BYSEL Trader — Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 19, 2026

Summary: BYSEL Trader is a stock market analysis and paper trading app. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. All portfolio and trading data is stored locally on your device.

1. Introduction

Welcome to BYSEL Trader ("we", "our", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application BYSEL Trader (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account required: The App does not require registration, login, or any personal details to use.
  • No personal data: We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • No location data: We do not access or track your location.
  • No contacts or photos: We do not access your contacts, camera, photos, or files.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your watchlist and portfolio (paper trading positions)
  • Price alert configurations
  • App preferences and theme settings
  • AI chat history during the current session

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third party. You can clear this data at any time by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the App.

4. Network Requests

The App connects to the internet solely to:

  • Fetch live stock market data: Current stock prices, historical data, and market information are retrieved from our backend server, which sources data from Yahoo Finance.
  • AI analysis: When you use the AI Assistant, your queries are sent to our backend server for processing. These queries contain only stock-related questions and are not linked to any personal identity.

No personal identifiers are sent with these requests. We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics SDKs.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party data sources:

  • Yahoo Finance: For retrieving stock market data. Yahoo Finance's own privacy policy applies to their data collection practices. We only retrieve publicly available market data.

We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

6. Advertising

The App does not display any advertisements. We do not use any advertising SDKs or ad networks.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. The App is intended for users who are of legal age to participate in stock market activities in their jurisdiction.

8. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store personal data on our servers, there is minimal risk of data breach affecting your personal information. All local data on your device is protected by your device's own security measures (PIN, fingerprint, encryption).

9. Financial Disclaimer

Important: BYSEL Trader is for educational and informational purposes only. The App provides paper trading (simulated trading) and AI-generated stock analysis. This is not financial advice. AI predictions and scores are based on technical analysis and historical data — they do not guarantee future performance. Always consult a qualified financial advisor before making real investment decisions.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
